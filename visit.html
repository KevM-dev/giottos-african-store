About Giottos.

We opened on Aylsham Road to bring a proper African grocery to the neighbourhood — the kind where you can pick up egusi, ugu, palm oil and suya pepper without trekking across the country.

We work with importers and small farms across ten countries to keep the shelves stocked with what you actually cook — not whatever's easiest to ship.
